Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026 · Developer: Marco Bargellini · Contact: grocerycart.support@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Lievitati Lab ("the App", "we", "our") is developed and operated by Marco Bargellini, an individual based in California, United States. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

  • Usage data — anonymous, aggregated information about how features are used (via Apple Analytics or Google Analytics, if enabled by you at the device level).
  • Purchase information — in-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • User-generated content — recipes, timelines, notes, and photos you create are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this content to our servers.
  • Support communications — if you contact us via email, we store your email address and message to respond to your request.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account credentials for use of the App.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use tracking technologies (cookies, pixels, fingerprinting) within the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

All content you create in the App is stored locally on your device using standard iOS and Android secure storage. We do not operate cloud servers that store your personal data. We take reasonable technical measures to protect any support communications you send us.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution and in-app purchases.
  • APNs / Firebase Cloud Messaging — for local notification delivery. No personal data is transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of personal data we hold about you (primarily support email records). To exercise these rights, contact us at grocerycart.support@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
